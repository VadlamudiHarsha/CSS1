About

Amazon Prime OTT is a streaming service that provides video-on-demand content with a monthly or annual subscription. OTT stands for over-the-top, which means that the content is provided via streaming services over the Internet and bypasses traditional distribution like cable. Amazon Prime Video Store is an example of a TVOD service, which allows viewers to either purchase the content for repeated viewing or rent it for a single-time use.
